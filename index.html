Standard Eco-Resort Features

All SunDrop resorts include these comprehensive sustainable living features

EcoHomes

Luxury sustainable homes with 1/3 to 1/2 acre density, built with eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient designs.

Farm-to-Fork Dining

21 family-style meals per week delivered to each EcoHome, prepared by on-site chefs using CoOp farm produce.

Holistic Wellness

Full-service spa, fitness center with yoga/spin classes, and personalized wellness programs for residents.

Community Services

15 hours/week personalized assistance including senior care, childcare, executive support, and tutoring.

Sustainable Energy

Reforestation
